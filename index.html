To save, open the in-game menu (Esc) and choose Save Game — progress is then kept in this browser. On phones & tablets the screen splits: the game on top, and a movement joystick + Shoot/Pogo/Jump below. Pogo fires Pogo+Jump together for the high super-bounce that's hard to time on touch.
